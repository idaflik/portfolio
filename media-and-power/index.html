media & power

Visual identity, posters and facebook graphics for a series of talks around power structures and media production

In 2019 I initiated and organized a series of talks called "Media & Power" together with Ariadna Carracosa, where we invited guest speakers for talks on power structures and imbalances, discrimination and (mis)representation in the realm of journalism and media production.

For this, I created a visual identity, posters and graphics for e-mail and facebook announcements.

You can find a basic documentation of the talks (and an audio recording of one of them) here.
