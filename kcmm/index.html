kids courage macht methoden

Design and layout for a book with exercises and thoughts on empowering, anti-discriminatory children‘s pedagogy

KidsCourage is a project working on democratic and anti-discriminatory informal education, children‘s rights and empowerment. In 2019, they published a book about their approach to pedagogy and a collection of their favourite exercises, which I created a layout design as well as illustrations for – based on a corporate identity created by Ipek Erdöl.
