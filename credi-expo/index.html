credi exhibition

A permanent DIY exhibition for showcasing and explaining a rural NGOs work to its visitors

Team: Rinos Affafa, Tereza Suchankova

As part of an internship with CREDI-ONG, our team conceptualized and created an exhibition about their work and missions. The outdoor exhibition was put up at the NGOs main site where visitors are frequently welcomed, while most of their projects are spread across different sites in the area and often times difficult to visit. We created a series of posters and some three-dimensional objects that illustrate the issues CREDI-ONG fights, as well as some games to engage younger visitors (and adults, too!).
