kinderkisten

Informative playgrounds-in-a-box for a museum

Team: gewerkdesign

For the „Bürgermuseum Wolfenbüttel“, a museum of the history of a small town in Germany, I helped conceptualize and create two little cabinets for kids. These cabinets explore the same topics as the adults read about in the exhibition, but from an kid‘s angle.

One of the two cabinets is about industrialization and shows the different manufacturing processes of mashed potatos in handmade and industrial productions.

The second cabinet is about the river flowing through the city and its importance for the same; this one asks visitors to find where different craftsmen worked and how that influenced the water quality.
