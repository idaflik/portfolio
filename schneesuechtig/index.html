schneesüchtig

Flyers for a ski club

Schneesüchtig is a group of ski and snowboard enthusiasts that each year organize a variety of different snow sports trips for a young audience. These flyers announcing the 2017/18 and 2018/19 programmes were based on photos taken on previous trips and Schneesüchtig‘s logo and identity colors.
