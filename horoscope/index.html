Weather horoscope

A humorous interactive installation

Team: Heinrich Ehnert

The weather horoscope foretells your future – always up-to-date, always accurate. You have to set your zodiac on the machine, which constantly measures current states of different environmental data and then deviates your personal horoscope based on this super-scientific data in combination with your zodiac.

Created in a one-week workshop that introduced the basics of Arduino and weather-data sensors.
