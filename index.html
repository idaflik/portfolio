about

Hi, I'm Ida!

Here, I collect some of my design work. I like to play both with analogue things like different materials and craft techniques as well as digital things like code. I like to work from an improvising, tinkering, low-tech and open-source based approach and currently I draw a lot of inspiration from data journalism, (visual) investigations and interactive objects and visualizations.

When I'm not working on design projects you may find me trying my best at open source reseach, learning to code websites and graphics, doing projects for my Investigative Journalism studies, engaging with political organizing or helping organize free time activites for kids and youth and facilitating different workshops.

Don't hesitate to get in touch!
