altona

Playful data translations and interactive elements for an exhibition about urban planning

Team: gewerkdesign

As part of my work at gewerkdesign, I helped develop analogue interactive elements aswell as playful data installations for an exhibition about living and urban planning in Hamburg. The concept entailed spreading furnished, monochrome rooms across the exhibition space, inside which different sub-topics of the (historical) exhibition were communicated with a contemporary focus, to engage visitors in a more playful way.
