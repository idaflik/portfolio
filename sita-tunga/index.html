sita & tunga

Two mascot figures for an NGO to use in various materials

Sita and Tunga are two mascots created from one: the Sitatunga antilope, name giver of the Sitatunga valley. During my internship at CREDI-ONG, an NGO that amongst other things aims to protect the area of the Sitatunga valley, I created these two fantasy-animals that will appear on various different print materials and signs of CREDI-ONG.
